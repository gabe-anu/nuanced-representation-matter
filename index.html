Why Diversity and Positive Representation is Important in Media

art imitates life and life imitates art

The purpose of the website is to hopefully illustrate how a nuanced dipiction, representation, and diversity of people especially marginlized indivduals in both media and entertainment is emprative to fosters media that not only resonates with audiences from different backgrounds but also helps in promoting better understanding, empathy, and appreciation for one another, while also contributing toward illustrating a nuanced and comprehensive portrayal of the world we live in.

Brith of a Nation

A film with everlasting impact

Negetive depictions of marginalized minority groups in media can have real-life consequences that alter the way society not only views them but also justification how they should treat members of such minority groups based on stereotypes created through media.

We must all act like said stereotypes ?

Negetive depiction of marginalized minority groups can leave members of such groups feeling as if they need to exist in a box based on what stereotypes have been applied to them negatively affecting the way they view themselves of they are unable to adhere to such standards.
